Welcome

The Macayieta Island Resort currently rated the best resort in the country welcomes you to its beautiful scenary

A stunning white beach fringed by coral reefs and a tidal saltwater lake surrounded by a mangrove forest.

A diverse mixture of indigenous and tropical Flora that attracts a wide variety of fascinating insect and primate life.

All this combined with the loving care of our Kenyan staff will make your holiday on Macayieta Island intriguing, relaxing and warmly memorable.

WE ARE OPEN ALL YEAR.
