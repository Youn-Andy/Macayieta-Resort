Activities

Unlike most other island destinations, Chale Island has something to offer everyone.

Normal island activities available include a magical SPA, a well equipped watersports center and a selection of boat excursions.

From Chale Island we can also offer you trips to Diani Beach Shopping Center and the famous Nomad Beach Bar, or for the more adventurous a wide range of safaris.

Diving

Diving lessons are offered to those who love the thrill of being underwater , by our executive class diving coaches
who are world class certified

For those who are not new to diving depending on the accomadation you chose you can either hire the diving gear
or it comes as a package deal with your room

Boat rides

Boat rides are offered and or you can hire a boat and go across to the amazing Poa Island that is just 10kilometres from
